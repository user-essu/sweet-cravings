Our Story

Baking happiness since 2018

From Our Kitchen to Your Heart

Sweet Cravings began as a small home kitchen experiment by founder Maria Santos in 2018. What started as weekend baking for family and friends quickly blossomed into a beloved local bakery known for its commitment to quality and authentic flavors.

We believe that great baking is both an art and a science. Every recipe has been meticulously developed and tested hundreds of times to achieve the perfect balance of texture, flavor, and presentation.

Today, our team of 12 passionate bakers continues to honor that original home-kitchen spirit—using time-honored techniques, refusing to compromise on ingredients, and treating every order as if it were for our own family.

Our Values

Quality First

We never use artificial preservatives or shortcuts. If we wouldn't serve it to our own children, we won't serve it to you.

Local Sourcing

We partner with local Filipino farmers and suppliers to source fresh eggs, dairy, and produce whenever possible.

Community

We donate unsold baked goods daily to local shelters and sponsor community events throughout the year.
